## Problem

Current analytics dashboard doesn't surface the right signals for engineering managers and incident commanders to understand performance trends, MTTR, and team health. Customers lack custom reporting. Redesign aims to make analytics a decision-making tool, not just a log.

## Target users

- Engineering managers
- Site Reliability Engineers (SREs)
- Enterprise admins wanting team-level incident performance data
