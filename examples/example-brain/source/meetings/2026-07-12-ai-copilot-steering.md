Meeting Notes — AI Copilot Steering Committee
Date: 2026-07-12

Attendees:
- Emma Rodriguez (VP Product)
- David Kim (Engineering Manager)
- Priya Nair (AI Engineering Lead)
- Olivia Brooks (Head of Customer Success)

Discussion:

Engineering completed an internal evaluation of AI Incident Copilot.

The live incident summarization model has improved significantly. Manual editing dropped from approximately 30% to 12% across the latest internal benchmark.

However, troubleshooting recommendations remain unreliable. Around 35% of suggested actions were considered misleading or incomplete during evaluation.

Decision:
The team agreed to postpone AI-generated troubleshooting recommendations from the V1 launch.

V1 will now include:
- Live incident summaries
- Similar historical incidents
- AI-generated postmortems

Troubleshooting recommendations move to V1.1 after additional model improvements.

Customer Success supports this decision because customers consistently value accuracy over feature breadth.

Engineering estimates this change reduces launch risk and keeps the current release schedule intact.

Action Items:
- Update AI Copilot scope.
- Record the launch decision.
- Create a hypothesis to validate whether customers still perceive strong value without troubleshooting recommendations.
